What is Session?

Session is a decentralized messaging app focused on providing maximum user privacy and anonymity. It uses a global network of community-operated nodes to route messages, ensuring that no central entity can access user metadata.

Unlike mainstream apps, Session's core architecture is designed to protect not just what you say, but who you say it to.

The Utility Flywheel

The SESH token's value is driven by its utility within the Session ecosystem. This flywheel model creates a sustainable economic loop where app usage directly benefits the network and its participants.

Staking

Operators stake 25,000 SESH to run a node, securing the network and earning rewards.

Session Name Service (SNS)

Users spend SESH to buy unique usernames. These tokens are burned, reducing supply.

Session Pro

The core revenue driver. Fiat payments for premium features are used to buy and burn SESH, with an equivalent amount re-minted to reward stakers.

How Session Pro Fuels the Network

The "burn-and-remint" mechanism is the heart of Session's DePIN economy. It translates real-world revenue from subscriptions into on-chain buy pressure and enhanced rewards for the node operators who power the network.

Step 1: User Pays

User subscribes to Session Pro with Fiat (USD, EUR) via App Store.

Step 2: Market Buy

A 3rd party uses the Fiat to buy SESH tokens on the open market.

Step 3: Burn

The purchased SESH tokens are programmatically burned.

Step 4: Remint

An equal amount of SESH is re-minted into the Staking Reward Pool.

Step 5: Reward

Node operators receive higher staking rewards, strengthening the network.
